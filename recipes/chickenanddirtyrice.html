Chicken & Dirty Rice

Chicken and sasuage cooked over top of rice in this one pot dish

This is one of our households staples. Chicken and sausage cooked over top of a bed of rice infuses tons of flavor into the dish.

Ingredients

Marinade

  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Rest of Dish

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 link of sausage
  • 4 bone-in skin on chicken thighs
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 1 japaleno pepper
  • 2 celery ribs
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  • pinch of red pepper flakes
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup dry long-grain rice
  • 2 1/4 cup chicken broth or water
  • sliced green onions for garnish

Steps

  1. Dice the onion, carrots and celery. Heat the butter in a dutch oven. Add the mirepoix and saute for 3 minutes.
  2. Add garlic and spices, saute about a minute.
  3. Add chicken stock, chicken and bay leaves.
  4. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer, covered, until the chicken is cooked through, about 20 minutes.
  5. Remove chicken and shred or dice.
  6. Bring the soup to a boil and add the noodles until tender, about 5 minutes.
  7. Add the chicken back to the soup. Taste for seasoning and add salt and pepper. Add the lemon.
  8. Serve with buns and enjoy!
